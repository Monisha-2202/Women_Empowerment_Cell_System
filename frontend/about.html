About the Women Empowerment Cell

Mission

The Women Empowerment Cell is committed to promoting gender equality, fostering leadership, and ensuring a safe and inclusive environment for all women in our institution. We aim to equip women with the confidence and resources needed to reach their full potential.

Vision

To create a world where women have equal access to opportunities, resources, and respect — both within and outside the academic space. Our vision is to foster an environment of empowerment, inclusivity, and dignity.

Objectives

  • To empower female students and staff through various programs and initiatives.
  • To address grievances and support women's rights and safety.
  • To organize workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns.
  • To promote leadership and decision-making roles among women.
